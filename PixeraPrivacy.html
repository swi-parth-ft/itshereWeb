1. Introduction

Welcome to Pixera. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Pixera does not collect, store, or process any personal user data. We do not use analytics, tracking technologies, or request user-identifiable information at any point within the app.

3. Purchases and Third-Party Services

Pixera uses RevenueCat solely for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes only the purchase-related metadata required by the App Store. We do not transmit or store any personal user data ourselves.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, trackers, or any other technology to monitor user activity inside or outside of Pixera.

5. Security

Since Pixera does not collect any user data, no personal data is stored or transmitted. All processing takes place locally on the device.

Pixera does not rely on any external servers or cloud services. Screenshots and edits remain on your device unless you explicitly export them.

6. Compliance

As no personal data is collected, Pixera complies with all applicable data protection and privacy regulations by design.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if our app behavior changes. The latest version will always be available within the app and on our website, with the "Last updated" date shown above.
