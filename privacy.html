1. Introduction

Welcome to it's here. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use our service. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal information is protected.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data from our users through our app.

  • Sign In with Apple: If you choose to sign in using Apple, only the minimal information required to authenticate your account is processed.
  • Public AR Maps: Collaborative AR Maps created within the app are stored in a public database and are accessible by anyone.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies on our app.

4. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Although we do not collect personal data:

  • Any data stored via iCloud is protected with standard iCloud encryption.
  • User PINs are secured using SHA256 encryption.

Please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services such as Sign In with Apple. These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them to understand how your information is managed by these providers.

6. Compliance

We strive to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations. As our app does not actively collect personal data, our compliance obligations are minimal. However, we remain committed to respecting your rights under relevant laws.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
