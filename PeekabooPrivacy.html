1. Introduction

Welcome to Peekaboo. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use our service. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal information is protected.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Peekaboo does not require or store user accounts, emails, or identifiers. The app operates entirely on-device and does not track or store any user data.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies on our app.

4. Security

Peekaboo does not collect or store user data. As such, there is no sensitive information transmitted or stored. All features function locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy.

5. Third-Party Services

Peekaboo uses RevenueCat for managing in-app purchases. RevenueCat handles transaction validation through Apple’s App Store infrastructure. We do not have access to any personal information through this integration.

6. Compliance

As Peekaboo does not collect any personal data, it aligns with privacy regulations such as GDPR and CCPA by design.
